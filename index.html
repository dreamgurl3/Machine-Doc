Instruction for Twin Tub Washing Machine

Overview

The Twin Tub washer machine is a two-sided semi-automatic washer machine that is hooked up to a hose.Most of people use this type of washing machine in Costa Rica as most of the time dryer is not needed because people can air dry their clothes. This type of machine you need to do most of the things manually. Typically, the left side is the tub to fill with water for washing and the right side to rinse and spin. The machine has a very simple lay out and simple turn knobs from left to right including:

  • Washing timer
  • Washing program
  • Water drain
  • Spin timer

Control Panel

  • Washing timer: Washing time is to set how many minutes to set the washing. Usually 12 minutes is sufficient.
  • Washing program: Washing program is for you to set whether is gentle, normal or heavy cycle.
  • Water drain: Water drain is for draining the water after washing is done.
  • Spin timer: Spin timer is for setting how many minutes to spin the clothes after it is finish washing.

Washing Method

To start the process of washing : You want to turn on the faucet to fill the tub with water.Itâ€™s a good idea to put detergent in the tub as you fill the tub with water to let the powder dissolve. Fill the water to a desire level, then you can put your clothes into the tub. If you need to add a bit more water into the tub, you can. Once your clothes are in, simply turn the washing time to desire minutes. Typically, 12 minutes is sufficient enough.

Rinsing Method 1

There are two ways of rinsing after washing your clothes. This one is to put as much clothes as it will fit into the spin tub and move the hose over to spin tub to rinse off the soap. Then close the inner safety lid tight and then the outer layer top and then set 3 minutes to spin.

Rinsing Method 2

The second way of rinsing is simply draining out the dirty water out of the tub and fill the tub with clean water to get the soap off the clothes. With this method, you just have to put the clothes over to the spin tub after getting the soap off the clothes.

Spin cycle

  1. Grab the clothes out of the washing tub and shake out your clothes as you are putting into the spin tub. Do not fill the tub super full.
  2. Make sure the clothes are evenly distributed.
  3. Turn the spin timer to 3 minutes. If you hear shaking sounds simply stop the timer and make sure the clothes are evenly distributed and then try again.
  4. Last thing is to hang the clothes out to dry.

Cautions

Below are some of the things you want to avoid doing:

  • Do not spray water directly to the machine.
  • Avoid installing the machine somewhere damp or rainy environment to avoid electric shock, fire, machine failure, or deformation.
  • Avoid dampness.
  • Do not dismantle the machine by yourself.
  • Do not insert or unplug the plug with wet hands to avoid electric shock.
  • Please connect grounding wire of the plug properly.
  • Do not place inflammable materials like burning candle, mosquito incense, cigarette etc. or heating source like electric stove, calorifier etc. on the machine.
